How to Play

Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

This is a simple game involving two players. The game is played as follows:

  1. Player 1 chooses between "rock", "paper" and "scissors"
  2. Player 2 chooses between "rock", "paper" and "scissors"

The winner of the game is decided according to the match up between the players' choices:

  • Rock blunts scissors to win the game.
  • Paper wraps rock to win the game.
  • Scissors cuts paper to win the game.
  • If both players make the same choice, the game results in a draw!
